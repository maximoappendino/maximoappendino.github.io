Preserving Lost Media

Old cartoons and movies hold a special place in my memory. Many of them shaped my imagination, humor, and sense of storytelling — yet some of these shows have completely vanished from the internet.

That disappearance is what pushed me to start a personal project focused on preserving lost media. Content that once aired on TV and meant something to people shouldn’t be erased just because it’s no longer profitable or popular.

Hosting and archiving old shows is my way of protecting cultural memories. It’s not just nostalgia — it’s preservation. Once something is lost, it rarely comes back.
